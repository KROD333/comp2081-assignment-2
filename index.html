Synopsis

Talbot weaves Bohm’s physics and Pribram’s neurotheory into an audacious holographic model of reality where mind, matter, space, and time are projections from a deeper order.

The book blends frontier science with paranormal case studies to suggest the universe behaves like a cosmic cinema shaped by consciousness.
